NOTICE - Compression Lab
========================

Contains fflate and brotli-wasm, vendored locally.

Vendored artifacts
------------------
fflate.min.js - fflate browser build used for gzip, zlib, raw deflate, and
matching decompression checks. fflate is licensed MIT.

brotli_wasm.js and brotli_wasm_bg.wasm - brotli-wasm browser build used for
Brotli compression and decompression checks. brotli-wasm is licensed
Apache-2.0.

Upstream projects
-----------------
- fflate: https://github.com/101arrowz/fflate (MIT)
- brotli-wasm: https://github.com/httptoolkit/brotli-wasm (Apache-2.0)

The app shell, measurement harness, entropy estimator, and UI are original
Rocket Pudding code.
